# Operating Rhythm

Use this repo as a weekly product signal review, not as a passive backlog.

## Monday

Read `outputs/founder_roadmap_memo.md`.

Decide which items need founder attention, product scope, customer validation, or non-product ownership.

## Tuesday

Review `outputs/roadmap_decision_queue.csv`.

Confirm:

- Build-now candidates
- Validate-next candidates
- Roadmap candidate list items
- Owner and due timing

## Wednesday

Run customer validation.

Use top feedback themes to plan calls with affected customers or prospects. Ask what problem the request solves, how often it happens, what workaround exists, and what business impact it creates.

## Thursday

Assign owners outside product.

Use `outputs/non_product_fix_queue.csv` to separate product work from onboarding, sales narrative, support, documentation, training, CS, pricing, or rejection.

## Friday

Update product tracker and weekly operating review.

Use `outputs/product_operating_review.md` to prepare the agenda. Move decisions into Linear, Jira, Productboard, Notion, Airtable, CRM, CS tracker, or the weekly review system your team already uses.
