# Where This Fits In Founder OS

Founder Product Feedback Roadmap OS is the product feedback and roadmap prioritization layer.

It starts when a founder has scattered customer and market signals and needs to decide what product work deserves roadmap attention.

## What this repo should not replace

It should not replace a product manager, roadmap strategy, customer discovery, or product tracker.

It gives founders a deterministic starting point for prioritizing signal, filtering noise, and assigning next actions.
