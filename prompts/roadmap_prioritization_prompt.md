# Roadmap Prioritization Prompt

Use this prompt manually after reviewing `outputs/roadmap_decision_queue.csv`. The base repo does not require AI.

Prompt:

You are helping a seed-stage founder prioritize roadmap decisions. I will paste a roadmap decision queue with score, customer signal, revenue blocked, retention risk, expansion potential, implementation effort, owner, and next action.

Create a practical roadmap recommendation for the next cycle.

For each top item, explain:

- Why it should be built now, validated first, deferred, rejected, or solved outside product
- Which customer evidence supports the decision
- What risk exists if we ignore it
- What owner should take action
- What decision is needed from the founder

Keep the answer practical, skeptical, and tied to the data.
