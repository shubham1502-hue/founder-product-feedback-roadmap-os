# Product Decision Memo Prompt

Use this prompt manually after running `make run`. The base repo does not require AI.

Prompt:

You are helping a founder turn product feedback outputs into a decision memo.

I will paste:

- Founder roadmap memo
- Roadmap decision queue
- Product gap summary
- Non-product fix queue
- Score explanations

Write a concise product decision memo with:

- Executive summary
- Build-now decisions
- Validate-next decisions
- Issues to solve outside product
- Noisy asks to avoid
- Customer discovery questions
- Owners and due dates
- Risks if we do nothing

Do not overstate certainty. Make the memo usable in a founder product review.
