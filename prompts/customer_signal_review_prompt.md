# Customer Signal Review Prompt

Use this prompt manually with `outputs/customer_signal_matrix.csv` and `outputs/account_feedback_view.csv`. The base repo does not require AI.

Prompt:

You are helping a founder review customer product signals across sales, onboarding, support, retention, expansion, and demos.

I will paste theme-level and account-level feedback outputs. Identify:

- Strong repeated signals
- Weak or noisy signals
- Enterprise-specific asks
- Broadly useful product gaps
- Retention-risk product gaps
- Expansion unlocks
- Non-product fixes
- Accounts that need founder or product follow-up

Return a short product review agenda and a 7-day action list.
